Digital Infotainment System

A project focused on participants of all age groups. My role involved User Experience, Interaction, and Interface Design.

I contributed to the development of a digital infotainment system that is user-friendly, intuitive, and above all, safe.

Develop

Iteration 1

I began this project by collecting data through the creation of a paper prototype, which served as a foundation for further development. I invited participants from various age groups to customize their dashboard. This process provided valuable insights into their preferences and similarities:

  • • All participants preferred a full overview map.
  • • All participants wanted their applications positioned on the left side of the screen.
  • • Younger participants (roughly 18–30 years old) saw more value in using applications and therefore opted for a more populated screen layout.

Iteration 2

During the second iteration, a mid-fidelity prototype was developed to give participants an impression of how their custom-built dashboards would look and feel in a digital environment.

In terms of functionality, I observed that many younger participants found it easy to navigate the system while driving. They remained satisfied with their initial setup choices, which allowed me to further refine and configure a solid infotainment system.

Iteration 3

The infotainment system required visual improvements, which led to a redesign based on user testing results. I streamlined the layout, removed irrelevant apps, and focused on the essentials by prioritizing safety throughout the design.

The updated design was considered elegant and user-friendly. Even older participants found it easy to use, although some mentioned they wouldn’t use features like making phone calls. I conducted further testing with a new group of participants, focusing on final refinements.

Deliver

The final week was dedicated to minor fixes, such as resolving bugs and further developing the system settings.

The final product aligned well with the original assignment. Many participants reported feeling safe while driving and using the infotainment system. This confirmed the success of the product for me — all essential features were present and easily accessible.
